IPTV Mine Pro is a clean, modern, and open-source IPTV player for Android and Android TV.

Features:
- Stream live TV channels, movies, and TV shows using M3U playlists.
- Premium, animated navigation UI with custom vector drawable icons.
- Built-in video player with media control options.
- Support for television devices with Leanback UI navigation.
- 100% open-source, private, and free of proprietary Google services/tracking.
